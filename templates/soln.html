The ML and Deep Vision-based solution was tested for detecting adulteration in Toor dal. Through field studies, surveys, and expert interviews, it was found that the implemented solution has better coverage of quality factors such as shape, size, and color, and analytics compared to commercial solutions in the market. The Machine Learning-based solution will result in maximum percentage of detection of the presence of Foreign Objects in Toor Dal batch samples to prevent adulteration and ensure consumer safety.
The solutions offered by Swan Sorter Systems, such as their color sorters and near-infrared (NIR) sorters, can also be used as complementary tools for identifying adulterants in Toor Dal samples. By combining the ML-based system with advanced sorting technology, the accuracy and efficiency of adulteration detection can be further improved, leading to better food safety and quality control in the Toor Dal industry.
